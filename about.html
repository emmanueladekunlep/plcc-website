About PLCC

Liberating Souls. Making Disciples. Fulfilling Destinies.

Who We Are

Prayer Life Christian Centre (PLCC) is a Spirit-empowered community raising praying, productive believers who transform nations. We are a family where purpose meets power.

We envision a church where lives are transformed by the power of God, where people encounter freedom from sin, bondage, and spiritual strongholds. Discipleship is intentional, relational, and rooted in God's Word — developing mature Christians who reflect Christ in character and influence.

A generation rising to walk boldly in purpose, activating spiritual gifts, maximizing potential, and fulfilling divine destiny in every sphere: family, ministry, marketplace, and nations.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel with power, win souls into the Kingdom, disciple believers through sound biblical teaching, and equip every member to discover, develop, and deploy their God-given gifts and destinies for the transformation of families, communities, and nations.

"Pray without ceasing... but also work with precision." – PLCC Motto

Our Core Values

Faith

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

Love

We are committed to loving God and loving people, creating a community where everyone belongs and is valued.

Community

We believe in the power of authentic relationships, intentional discipleship, and walking together in purpose.

Purpose

We equip believers to discover, develop, and deploy their God-given gifts and destinies for kingdom impact.

Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion and the baptism of the Holy Spirit.

Healing

We believe in divine healing and the operation of the gifts of the Holy Spirit.

Second Coming

We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of the dead.
