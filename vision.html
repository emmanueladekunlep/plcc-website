Our Vision & Mandate

Liberating Souls. Making Disciples. Fulfilling Destinies.

Our Vision

"To raise a Spirit-empowered community committed to liberating souls from darkness, making disciples of Jesus Christ, and equipping believers to discover, develop, and fulfil their God-given destinies."

Liberation: Luke 4:18 · John 8:36 · Acts 26:18
Discipleship: Matthew 28:19-20 · Colossians 1:28
Destiny: Jeremiah 1:5 · Ephesians 2:10

Our Kingdom Mandate

Biblical foundations that drive every ministry, every pastor, every outreach.

Liberating Souls

Luke 4:18 · John 8:36 · Acts 26:18

Proclaiming freedom from sin, bondage, and spiritual strongholds. Every soul set free transforms families and communities. We are called to be agents of liberation, breaking chains and setting captives free through the power of the Gospel.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Evangelism and Outreach
  • Freedom and Deliverance
  • Community Transformation

Making Disciples

Matthew 28:19-20 · Colossians 1:28

Intentional, relational discipleship rooted in God's Word, producing mature believers who reflect Christ in character and influence. We are committed to raising disciples who make disciples.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Bible Study and Teaching
  • Mentorship and Coaching
  • Leadership Development

Fulfilling Destinies

Jeremiah 1:5 · Ephesians 2:10

Equipping believers to discover and deploy their God-given gifts, transforming the marketplace, nations, and every sphere of life. We believe every believer has a destiny to fulfill.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Gift Discovery and Activation
  • Marketplace Ministry
  • Purpose and Destiny Coaching

Why This Mandate Matters

The Great Commission is not just a command — it is our calling. Every believer is called to liberate souls, make disciples, and fulfill their destiny. PLCC exists to equip you for this purpose.

Global Impact
