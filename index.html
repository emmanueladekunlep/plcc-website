About PLCC

We envision a church where lives are transformed by the power of God, where people encounter freedom from sin, bondage, and spiritual strongholds. Discipleship is intentional, relational, and rooted in God's Word — developing mature Christians who reflect Christ in character and influence.

A generation rising to walk boldly in purpose, activating spiritual gifts, maximizing potential, and fulfilling divine destiny in every sphere: family, ministry, marketplace, and nations.
